Waymaker

The smart travel solution for everyone

The Problem

Process for creating and sharing a trip plan is time consuming for many users. Also unexpected things can happen during the trip and cause unnecessary stress

The Solution

Waymaker is an Ai based travel app that helps users plan and enjoy their trip easily and with peace of mind

Research

At the discovery phase of my project, I conducted user interviews in order to get a better understanding of the problem. The interviews and survey were conducted to find out people's travel preferences in general.

Findings from Research

01

Users want the process of planning and sharing the trip schedule to be easy and effortless.

02

The majority of travelers plan their trips in detail and encountering surprises such as bad weather and heavy traffics during travel can be extremely stressful.

03

There is a strong need to have a sense of safety during the pandemic in order to travel with comfort.

Prototyping - Design Timeline

I started the design process by sketching the basic layout. And then I created the Low-Fidelity prototype so I could execute user testing and iterate through many design options based on the tests. Once the usability test was finished, I started designing the Hi-Fidelity Mock-ups.

User Testing

A round of testing was needed in order to reveal possible usability problems.

Final Product & Thoughts

This was one of the projects I worked on during my studies at Georgia Institution of Technology UX/UI Bootcamp. One of the things that I learned during the process was that there is always room for improvements when it comes to the user experience of any product or process. I found myself reiterating and making changes a lot throughout the process.
